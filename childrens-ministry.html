Children's Ministry

Children are not the future of DOXA Sanctuary — they are the present. And they are loved, valued, and fully welcome here.

Our Programs Register Your Child

Who We Are

A Safe Place to Know God, Have Fun & Grow

Our Children's Ministry is a safe, fun, and Spirit-filled environment where children from infants to pre-teens encounter God's love and learn His Word. We are passionate about planting the seeds of faith early, because what is sown in a child's heart today will bear fruit for generations to come. Every child who walks through our doors is seen, valued, and deeply loved.

Programs

Something for Every Age

We have age-appropriate programs running every Sunday so the whole family can experience God together.

Birth – Age 3

Nursery

A warm, loving, and safe space for your littlest ones. Our dedicated nursery team provides attentive care for infants and toddlers while parents enjoy the service. Your baby is in good hands.

Every Sunday · During Service
Ages 4 – 8

Children's Church

A fun, gospel-centred program where preschoolers through early elementary children worship, discover Bible stories, and learn who they are in Christ through engaging, hands-on activities made just for them.

Every Sunday · During Service
Ages 9 – 12

Pre-Teen Ministry

Designed for older children who are ready to go deeper. We engage pre-teens with age-relevant Bible teaching, leadership opportunities, and meaningful conversations about faith, identity, and purpose in God.

Every Sunday · During Service

What We Do

How We Raise the Next Generation

Every Sunday we create an experience that kids actually look forward to.

Bible Teaching

Age-appropriate lessons straight from the Word of God, taught in ways children understand and remember.

Praise & Worship

Children learn to worship from an early age — with joy, energy, and a heart for God's presence.

Creative Activities

Crafts, drawing, and creative projects that reinforce biblical truths in a fun and memorable way.

Games & Fellowship

Building friendships and community through games and activities that make church a place kids love.

Our Team

Meet the Ministry Leader

Deaconess Margaret Kumi

Children's Ministry Leader

Deaconess Margaret Kumi leads our Children's Ministry with a deep love for children and an unwavering commitment to seeing the next generation encounter God. With patience, creativity, and a mother's heart, she has built a ministry where every child feels seen, celebrated, and spiritually nourished. Her team works faithfully every Sunday to create an environment where faith is planted and God's love is made real to every child in their care.

For Parents

What to Know Before You Arrive

We want your family's first Sunday to be smooth, comfortable, and full of joy.

Warm Welcome

When you arrive, our welcome team will personally guide you and your children to the right area. No stress, no confusion — just a warm hand to show you the way.

Safe Environment

The safety and wellbeing of your children is our highest priority. All our children's workers are trusted, vetted members of the DOXA Sanctuary family.

Partner With You

We see ourselves as partners with parents — not replacements. Our goal is to reinforce at church what you are already building at home in the Word and in faith.

Bring Them Along

Your Kids Will Love It Here

We know that as a parent, one of the most important decisions you make is where your children grow spiritually. At DOXA Sanctuary, we take that trust seriously. From the moment your child walks in, they will be greeted with love, introduced to God's Word, and surrounded by a team that genuinely cares about their growth.

Don't leave the kids at home — bring them. Church is better when the whole family comes together, and we have a place ready just for them.
