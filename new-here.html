Your First Sunday

What to Expect When You Arrive

Here's a simple walkthrough of what your first visit at DOXA Sanctuary will look like.

Find Us

We meet at 865 Morris Park Ave, Bronx, NY 10462. Street parking is available nearby. Arrive a few minutes early to settle in comfortably.

Be Welcomed

Our friendly welcome team will greet you at the door, answer any questions, and help you find a seat. You will never feel alone walking in.

Worship Together

We begin with powerful praise and worship. Feel free to sing along, clap, or simply soak it in. There is no pressure, just freedom to encounter God.

Hear the Word

Our pastor delivers a practical, Spirit-led message straight from the Bible. You will leave feeling encouraged, challenged, and full of hope.

Dress Code

Come Exactly As You Are

There is no dress code at DOXA Sanctuary. We believe God looks at the heart, not the outfit. Whether you come in your Sunday best or your everyday clothes, you are welcome here. What matters most is that you show up.

Our congregation is beautifully diverse, a mix of cultures, backgrounds, ages, and styles. You will fit right in. We are a family, and families welcome each other just as they are.
