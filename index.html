Who We Are

A Sanctuary Built for Every Person

DOXA Sanctuary is a Spirit-filled, apostolic church planted in the heart of the Bronx, New York, and a proud branch of TOP Ministries International. DOXA, the Greek word for Glory, is not just our name, it is our assignment. We exist to express the glory of God in worship, in leadership, in outreach, and in the everyday lives of the people He has entrusted to us.

We are a church where the Holy Spirit moves freely, where the Word of God is preached without compromise, and where every single person who walks through our doors is seen, valued, and equipped to fulfill their God-given calling. From raising Spirit-filled leaders to reaching the world through missions, the Bronx is our starting point, and the nations are our mission field.
