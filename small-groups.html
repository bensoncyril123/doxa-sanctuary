Why Small Groups?

Church Is Better Together

Sunday service is where we gather as one body, but small groups are where real life happens. They are where you are known by name, prayed for by people who care, and challenged to grow. At DOXA Sanctuary, our small groups are not programs; they are families within the family.

Whether you're new to faith or have walked with God for years, there is a group for you. Choose the one that fits your season, your interests, or your calling, and show up ready to grow.

Find Your Group

Our Small Groups

Six intentional communities, each one a place to belong, grow, and make a difference.

Faith

Bible Study & Discussion

Dive deep into God's Word in a relaxed, interactive setting. We explore books of the Bible, unpack Sunday sermons, and apply truth to everyday life. No Bible degree required, just a hunger to know God more.

  • Weekly · Weekday evenings
  • Open to all ages
  • In person & Zoom
Join This Group
Prayer

Prayer Circles

A small, intimate group built entirely around prayer. We come together to worship, intercede for one another, and pray for our church, city, and nation. These groups build some of the deepest spiritual bonds you'll ever experience.

  • Weekly · Early mornings or evenings
  • 5 – 12 people per circle
  • In person & Zoom
Join This Group
Life Stage

Life Stage Groups

People open up more when they feel understood. Our life stage groups bring together people in similar seasons, Young Adults, Married Couples, Men's Group, and Women's Group. Show up as you are and find people who get it.

  • Bi-weekly or monthly
  • Grouped by life season
  • In person · Bronx
Join This Group
Growth

Accountability & Growth

Small, focused, and intentional. These groups of 3 to 5 people commit together to spiritual discipline, consistent prayer, daily Bible reading, and personal growth goals. If you want to go deep, this is the group for you.

  • Weekly · Flexible times
  • 3 – 5 people per group
  • In person or Zoom
Join This Group
Leadership

Leadership Development

Built for those who sense a call to lead. This group develops servant leaders through teaching, mentorship, and hands-on ministry training. Learn how to lead prayer, facilitate discussions, and eventually shepherd your own group.

  • Bi-weekly · Evenings
  • Application-based · Limited spots
  • In person · Bronx

Outreach

Outreach & Evangelism

For those who want to take the Gospel beyond the walls of the church. This group goes out together, into neighborhoods, schools, and streets of the Bronx, to pray, serve, and invite people to encounter Jesus.

  • Monthly outreach days
  • Open to all
