Our Ministries

Every person has a place at DOXA Sanctuary. Find the ministry where you belong and make a difference.

Built for Every Person

A Ministry for Every Stage of Life

At DOXA Sanctuary, we believe the Church grows when every member is activated in their gift. Our ministries are not just programs, they are families within the family, each one intentionally designed to nurture, develop, and deploy every person into their God-given purpose. Wherever you are in life, there is a place for you here.

Empowering young people to live boldly for Christ

Women's Ministry

Our Four Ministries

Led by dedicated servants of God, each ministry is a community where you will grow, be challenged, and find your purpose.

Children's Ministry

Led by Deaconess Margaret Kumi

Our Children's Ministry is a safe, fun, and Spirit-filled environment where children from infants to pre-teens encounter God's love and learn His Word. We are passionate about planting the seeds of faith early, because we believe that what is sown in a child's heart today will bear fruit for generations to come.

Through engaging Bible lessons, praise and worship, games, and creative activities, we raise children who know who they are in Christ, love the presence of God, and grow up with a strong spiritual foundation. Every child who walks through our doors is seen, valued, and loved.

Ages: Birth to 12 Get Involved

Youth & Young Adults

Led by Mr. Daniel Asiamah

The Youth Ministry at DOXA Sanctuary is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community for teenagers and young adults who are ready to go beyond religion and into a real relationship with God. We meet young people exactly where they are, and challenge them to become who God created them to be.

Through worship nights, Bible studies, leadership training, and fellowship, we build young men and women who are bold in their faith, grounded in the Word, and equipped to impact their schools, campuses, and communities. The next generation of leaders is being raised right here.

Ages: 13 to 35 Get Involved

Men's Ministry

Led by Presiding Elder Aboagye

The Men's Ministry at DOXA Sanctuary is committed to raising men who are strong in faith, grounded in Scripture, and faithful in every area of life. We believe the strength of the family, the church, and the community is directly tied to the spiritual health of its men, and we take that seriously.

Through fellowship, accountability, discipleship, and practical teaching, we build men who lead their homes with wisdom, serve their church with humility, and walk in the integrity that God requires. Iron sharpens iron, and in this ministry, you will find brothers who sharpen you.

Open to all men Get Involved

Women's Ministry

Led by Dr. Mrs. Doreen Owusu

The Women's Ministry at DOXA Sanctuary is a community of women who are walking boldly in grace, purpose, and the power of the Holy Spirit. Under the leadership of Dr. Mrs. Doreen Owusu, this ministry creates a space where every woman is seen, celebrated, and equipped to fulfil her God-given calling.

Through retreats, Bible studies, mentorship, and powerful gatherings, we nurture women in every season of life, from the young woman finding her identity to the seasoned believer walking in legacy. You will find sisterhood, strength, and the presence of God in this ministry.
