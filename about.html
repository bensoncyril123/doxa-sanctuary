Our Story

Born Out of a Vision,
Planted in the Bronx

DOXA Sanctuary is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church located in the Bronx, New York, proudly part of the global family of TOP Ministries International. We are not just a congregation; we are a movement of believers who have been called, commissioned, and sent to bring the glory of God to one of the most dynamic cities in the world.

"DOXA means Glory, and that is the very heartbeat of everything we do."

From the Bronx to the Nations

As a branch of TOP Ministries International, we are rooted in a legacy of apostolic vision, sound doctrine, and Spirit-empowered ministry. We stand on the shoulders of a ministry that has touched countless lives across the globe, and we carry that same fire right here in New York.

Our Foundation

The Mandate of DOXA Sanctuary

Five non-negotiable pillars that define who we are and what we stand for as a church. This is the heartbeat of everything we do.

01

Souls

Every soul matters to God, and therefore every soul matters to us. Salvation is our highest calling.

02

Apostles' Doctrine

Sound, apostolic doctrine that produces genuine disciples. Truth is our anchor, not tradition or opinion.

03

Pure Worship

Authentic, Spirit-led worship that is fully surrendered to God's presence, not a performance.

04

Leadership

The Church rises and falls on its leaders. We develop servant leaders of integrity, humility, and Holy Spirit power.

05

Missions

From the Bronx to the ends of the earth. Missions is not a department, it is our identity.

What Drives Us

Our Four Convictions

Every service, every program, and every outreach at DOXA Sanctuary flows from these four foundational convictions.

CONVICTION 01

Raising Spirit-Filled Leaders

We are intentional about identifying, training, and releasing men and women who carry the Spirit of God into every sphere of life. Every person who walks through our doors is a leader in the making.

CONVICTION 02

Missions & Outreach

The Great Commission is not a suggestion, it is a mandate. From street evangelism in the Bronx to global missions through TOP Ministries International, the Bronx is our starting point and the world is our mission field.

CONVICTION 03

Holy Ghost-Impacted Services

Every service is designed for one thing, an encounter with the living God. Spirit-led worship, unadulterated Word, and unceasing prayer are not programs; they are our heartbeat.

CONVICTION 04

A Place Where Everyone Belongs

DOXA Sanctuary is a place of safety, belonging, and encounter. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned saint, there is a seat at the table for you. You were made to participate in the glory of God.
