Who We Are

Our Story

Built on Faith,
Rooted in Love

DOXA Sanctuary was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a space where every person can encounter the living God, find authentic community, and be equipped to live out their calling.

What started as a small gathering of 40 people in a rented school gymnasium has grown into a thriving church family of over 2,400 members, with 30+ active ministries serving our city and the world.

The name DOXA — the Greek word for "Glory" — captures everything we are about. We exist to give glory to God in every area of life: worship, service, family, work, and community.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

Everything we do is shaped by these four convictions.

01

The Word

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God. Sound teaching is the foundation of everything we do at DOXA Sanctuary.

02

Prayer

We are a house of prayer. We believe that prayer moves mountains and that a prayerful church is a powerful church. We pursue God's presence daily.

03

Community

We were not made to do life alone. Authentic relationships are central to who we are. Everyone belongs here, and everyone has a role to play.

04

Mission

We are called to reach the lost — in our city and around the world. We are passionate about evangelism, outreach, and global missions.
