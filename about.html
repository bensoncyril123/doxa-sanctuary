Our Story

Born Out of a Vision,
Planted in the Bronx

DOXA Sanctuary is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church located in the Bronx, New York, proudly part of the global family of TOP Ministries International. We are not just a congregation; we are a movement of believers who have been called, commissioned, and sent to bring the glory of God to one of the most dynamic cities in the world.

The name DOXA comes from the Greek word meaning "Glory," and that is the very heartbeat of everything we do. We exist to express God's glory in worship, in service, in leadership, and in every corner of our community. From the streets of the Bronx to the nations of the earth, DOXA Sanctuary carries a mandate that is both local and global.

As a branch of TOP Ministries International, we are rooted in a legacy of apostolic vision, sound doctrine, and Spirit-empowered ministry. We stand on the shoulders of a ministry that has touched countless lives across the globe, and we carry that same fire right here in New York.

What Drives Us

Four Pillars of Our Ministry

Every service, every program, and every outreach at DOXA Sanctuary flows from these four foundational commitments.

PILLAR 01

Raising Spirit-Filled Leaders for Impact

We believe that the Church is only as strong as its leaders. At DOXA Sanctuary, we are intentional about identifying, training, and releasing men and women who carry the Spirit of God into every sphere of life, the home, the workplace, the community, and beyond.

Through targeted workshops, mentorship programs, and hands-on ministry training, we equip believers to move beyond the pew and into their God-given purpose. We don't just produce churchgoers, we produce world changers. Every person who walks through our doors is a leader in the making, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our training is not merely academic. It is Spirit-led, Word-grounded, and practically oriented, because we believe that anointed leaders with both character and competence are what this generation desperately needs.

PILLAR 02

Reaching the World Through Missions & Outreach

The Great Commission is not a suggestion, it is a mandate. DOXA Sanctuary takes seriously the call to go into all the world and preach the Gospel. We are a missions-minded church, actively engaged in both local outreach in the Bronx and global missions that extend our reach across continents.

Right here in our neighborhood, we serve our community through street evangelism, food drives, community events, and practical acts of love that open doors for the Gospel. We believe that meeting a man's physical need creates an opportunity to meet his deepest spiritual need.

Globally, through our connection with TOP Ministries International, we partner with missionaries, support church planting efforts, and mobilize teams to carry the message of salvation to unreached people groups. The Bronx is our starting point, the world is our mission field.

PILLAR 03

Holy Ghost-Impacted Services

Every service at DOXA Sanctuary is designed with one goal in mind, an encounter with the living God. We do not come together simply to perform a religious ritual; we come to meet with the Holy Spirit and allow Him to move freely among His people.

Quality Praise & Worship

Our worship is not background music, it is a spiritual weapon and a gateway into God's presence. We give our best to God in every song, every sound, and every moment of praise.

Unadulterated Word

We are committed to preaching the whole counsel of God, uncut, uncompromised, and unapologetically biblical. We will never water down the truth to make it comfortable.

Spirit-Led Preaching

We preach the Spirit, not merely the letter, because as Scripture declares, the letter kills but the Spirit gives life. We believe in preaching that carries divine breath and produces genuine transformation.

Unceasing Prayer

Prayer is the engine of everything we do. We are a house of prayer, and we cultivate a culture of persistent, faith-filled intercession that saturates every ministry and every decision.

PILLAR 04

A Sanctuary Where Every Person Is Seen

DOXA Sanctuary is exactly what its name implies, a sanctuary. A place of safety, belonging, and encounter. We are deeply committed to building a community where no one is invisible, no one is overlooked, and no one is left behind.

We believe that every person who steps through our doors carries unique, God-given gifts, graces, and callings. Our role as a church is not to produce a congregation of passive spectators, but to tap into and activate every grace that God has deposited upon every individual within our walls.

Whether you are a first-time visitor, a new believer, or a seasoned saint, there is a seat at the table for you at DOXA Sanctuary. We will walk with you, pray with you, and help you discover and deploy every gift God placed inside of you. You were not made to sit on the sideline, you were made to participate in the glory of God.

Our Foundation

The Mandate of DOXA Sanctuary

Five non-negotiable pillars that define who we are and what we stand for as a church. This is the heartbeat of everything we do.

01

Souls

Every soul matters to God, and therefore every soul matters to us. The salvation of the lost is our highest calling and our greatest joy. We will never stop pursuing the one.

02

Apostles' Doctrine

We are committed to the teachings of the early church, sound, apostolic doctrine that produces genuine disciples, not merely religious attendees. Truth is our anchor.

03

Pure Worship

We pursue worship that is authentic, Spirit-led, and God-centered. Pure worship is not about performance, it is about a heart fully surrendered to the presence of God.

04

Leadership

We believe the Church rises and falls on its leadership. We are intentional about developing servant leaders who lead with integrity, humility, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

05

Missions

From the Bronx to the uttermost parts of the earth, we are a sent people. Missions is not a department, it is our identity. We exist to extend the Kingdom of God beyond every border.
