Volunteer

Use your gifts to serve God and make a difference in the lives of others.

Why Serve?

Every Gift Has a Place

At DOXA Sanctuary, we believe every believer is called to serve. When you volunteer, you are not just helping run a program. You are building the church, touching lives, and fulfilling your God-given purpose. There is a team for you, and we would love to have you on it.

Get Involved

Volunteer Teams

Find your team and start making an impact every Sunday.

Nursery

Care for our youngest members during Sunday service so their parents can worship freely. If you have a heart for babies and toddlers, this team is for you.

Children's Ministry

Teach, mentor, and pour into children aged 3 to 12 through Bible lessons, games, and creative activities. Help lay a faith foundation that lasts a lifetime.

Media & Slides

Run the presentation slides during Sunday service: lyrics, scriptures, and announcements. Keep the congregation connected to every moment of worship.

Sound Team

Operate the sound board and ensure every voice and instrument is heard clearly. Your work behind the scenes sets the atmosphere for the entire service.

Welcome Team

Be the first smile someone sees when they walk through our doors. Greet, usher, and help guests and members feel at home from the moment they arrive.

Cleaning & Facilities

Keep our sanctuary clean, orderly, and ready for worship. A tidy house honours God and creates a welcoming environment for everyone who walks in.
