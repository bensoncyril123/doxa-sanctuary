Who We Are

Beyond Religion — Into a Real Relationship

The Youth & Young Adults Ministry at DOXA Sanctuary is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community for teenagers and young adults who are ready to go beyond religion and into a real relationship with God. We meet young people exactly where they are and challenge them to become who God created them to be. This is not a social club — this is a movement.

What We Do

How We Build the Next Generation of Leaders

Every gathering is designed to ignite faith, build character, and release purpose.

Worship Nights

Powerful, Spirit-led worship experiences where young people encounter the presence of God deeply and authentically.

Bible Studies

Deep, relevant Word-based teaching that equips young believers to think biblically and live with conviction.

Leadership Training

Intentional development programs that identify, train, and release emerging leaders into their God-given calling.

Fellowship & Community

A genuine community where friendships are formed, accountability is built, and no one walks alone.

Ages 13 – 35

A Community That Believes in You

We believe the next generation is not a problem to be managed — they are a force to be released. At DOXA Sanctuary Youth & Young Adults, you will find a community that takes your faith seriously, challenges you to grow, and stands beside you through every season of life.

Whether you are in high school, navigating college, building a career, or finding your footing in adulthood, there is a place for you here. We will walk with you, pray with you, and help you discover the fullness of what God has placed inside of you.
