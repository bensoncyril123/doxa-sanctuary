Meet Our Leaders

Servants of God, leading with love, wisdom, and the Spirit of God.

Rev. Alex Owusu Jnr.

Lead Pastor

Rev. Alex Owusu Jnr. shepherds DOXA Sanctuary with love, wisdom, and apostolic vision, a preacher of the Word and a mentor committed to seeing every believer step into their God-given purpose.

Dr. Mrs. Doreen Owusu

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry Leader

Dr. Mrs. Doreen Owusu leads with grace, wisdom, and a mother's heart, overseeing Women's Ministry and pastoral care with a deep love for every soul she serves.

Presiding Elder Aboagye

Presiding Elder & Men's Ministry Leader

Presiding Elder Aboagye leads with strength, integrity, and a father's heart, overseeing the eldership and Men's Ministry, building men who are grounded in Scripture and faithful in every area of life.

Elder Johnson Yeboah

Elder & Marriage Counsellor

Elder Johnson Yeboah is a deeply honourable servant of God, trusted for his wisdom and compassion. He dedicates himself to marriage counselling, pastoral care, and the wellbeing of every family at DOXA Sanctuary.

Elder Edmond Yeboah

Elder & Bible Teaching

Elder Edmond Yeboah is a devoted student and teacher of the Word, committed to grounding the church in deep, sound biblical truth. His passion for Scripture shapes a congregation that not only hears the Word but lives by it.
